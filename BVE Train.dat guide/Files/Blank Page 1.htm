#ACCELERATION

0.6,0.5,16,24,1       = Throttle step 1.

0,0,0,0,1                = Throttle step 2.

0,0,0,0,1                = Throttle step 3.

0,0,0,0,1                = Throttle step 4.

0,0,0,0,1                = Throttle step 5.

0,0,0,0,1                = Throttle step 6.

0,0,0,0,1                = Throttle step 7.

0,0,0,0,1                = Throttle step 8.

 

 

 

0.6, = Minimum acceleration rate (km/h/s) for this notch.

0.5, = Maximum acceleration rate (km/h/s)  for this notch.

16, = Minimum attainable speed (km/h) for this notch (on level track).

24, = Maximum attainable speed (km/h) for this notch (on level track).

1, = Rate at which the acceleration fades away (0>1 older files, 1>4 newer files).
