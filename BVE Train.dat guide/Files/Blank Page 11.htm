#MOTOR_P1 = Plays when you apply power and the reverser is in forward (F) or backward (B) mode.

0,100,127 

0,100,…

 

#MOTOR_P2 = Plays when you apply power and the reverser is in forward (F) or backward (B) mode.

0,100,127 

0,100,…

 

#MOTOR_B1 = Plays when you apply brakes and the reverser is in forward (F) or backward (B) mode.

0,100,127 

0,100,…

 

#MOTOR_B2 = Plays when you apply brakes and the reverser is in forward (F) or backward (B) mode.

0,100,127 

0,100,…

 

0,       = Motorx wave file number (-1 = none).

100,    = Pitch of wave file.

127,    = Volume of wave file.

 

NOTE: Each section must have 800 lines and I would suggest editing the sound section using Mr Mackoys train editor, to avoid errors and incorrect playing of the sounds.
